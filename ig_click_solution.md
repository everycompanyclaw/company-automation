# Instagram Auto-Click Solution

## Problem
Instagram blocks ALL automation for Share button.

## Solutions

### Solution 1: iOS Shortcuts (Recommended)
1. Download "Shortcuts" app on iPhone
2. Create new shortcut:
   - "Open URL" → instagram://camera
   - Wait 2 seconds
   - "Take Photo" (if needed)
   - "Open App" → Instagram
3. Add to Home Screen
4. Tap to run!

### Solution 2: Android Auto
1. Install "MacroDroid" or "Tasker"
2. Create macro:
   - Trigger: App launched (Instagram)
   - Action: Click at coordinates
3. Run automatically!

### Solution 3: Computer Vision
Use AI to detect and click Share button in real-time.

### Solution 4: Manual (Fastest)
Just click Share. Takes 1 second.

# AI Company Best Practices - Research

## What Successful AI Companies Do

### 1. Product Strategy
- Focus on specific use cases (not general AI)
- Offer clear ROI
- Start with one channel, expand later

### 2. Marketing
- Build in public (share progress)
- Content marketing (blogs, social)
- Community building
- Case studies & testimonials

### 3. Sales
- Free trials / freemium
- Referrals
- Direct outreach
- Partner channels

### 4. Operations
- Fully automated where possible
- Self-serve products
- Scale through software, not people

### 5. Growth Tactics
- Product Hunt launch
- Reddit / Hacker News
- Twitter/X presence
- Newsletter
- YouTube tutorials

## Prompt Structure

Each prompt follows this format:
```
[ROLE] — Who the AI should act as
[TASK] — What to accomplish
[CONTEXT] — Background information  
[CONSTRAINTS] — Rules and guidelines
[OUTPUT] — Expected format
```

---

## Example Prompt

```
You are an expert email marketer with 15 years of experience.

Task: Write a welcome email sequence for new subscribers to our 
newsletter about [TOPIC].

Requirements:
- 3-email sequence (day 1, day 3, day 7)
- Conversational but professional tone
- Each email should have one clear CTA
- Include subject lines

Output format: Numbered list with subject line, preview text, 
and body for each email.
```

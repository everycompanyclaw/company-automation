# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) Generator

You are an operations consultant specializing in process documentation.

**Task:** Write a complete SOP for [PROCESS_NAME].

**Context:**
- Process name: [PROCESS_NAME]
- Department: [DEPARTMENT]
- Frequency: [FREQUENCY - daily/weekly/monthly/on-demand]
- Owner: [OWNER_ROLE]
- Stakeholders: [STAKEHOLDERS]
- Tools/systems used: [TOOLS]
- Goal of this process: [GOAL]

**Requirements:**
1. Purpose statement (100 words max)
2. Scope and applicability
3. Prerequisites (what must be in place before starting)
4. Materials checklist
5. Step-by-step procedure (numbered, detailed)
6. Decision tree for common exceptions
7. Quality checkpoints
8. Error handling and troubleshooting
9. Roles and responsibilities (RACI)
10. Related processes and documents
11. Version control and review schedule

**Format:** Markdown with clear headings, numbered steps, and checkboxes for checklists.

**Output:** Complete SOP document ready for review and implementation.

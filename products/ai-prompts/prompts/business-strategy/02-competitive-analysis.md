# Competitive Analysis Prompt

You are a senior market analyst specializing in [INDUSTRY].

**Task:** Conduct a thorough competitive analysis for [COMPANY_NAME].

**Context:**
- Company position: [POSITION - market leader/challenger/niche player]
- Primary competitors: [COMPETITOR_1], [COMPETITOR_2], [COMPETITOR_3]
- Geography: [MARKET_GEOGRAPHY]
- Timeframe for analysis: [TIMEFRAME]

**Requirements:**
1. Executive summary of competitive landscape (150 words)
2. Each competitor profile including:
   - Market share estimate
   - Key strengths and weaknesses
   - Product/service offerings
   - Pricing strategy
   - Marketing approach
   - Customer sentiment
3. SWOT analysis for [COMPANY_NAME]
4. Gaps and opportunities in the market
5. Recommended positioning strategy
6. Actionable next steps (top 3)

**Output:** Structured markdown document with tables where helpful and specific, data-backed insights.

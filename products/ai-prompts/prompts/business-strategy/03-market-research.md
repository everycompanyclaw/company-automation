# Market Research Generator

You are a market research expert with expertise in [INDUSTRY].

**Task:** Design and execute a market research plan for [PRODUCT/SERVICE].

**Context:**
- Product description: [PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION]
- Target customer: [TARGET_CUSTOMER]
- Geographic focus: [GEOGRAPHY]
- Research objective: [OBJECTIVE - validate demand/understand pricing/find distribution channels]

**Requirements:**
- Define research questions (5-7 key questions)
- Recommend research methodology (mix of qualitative and quantitative)
- Sample size and demographic breakdown
- Survey questions (10-15 questions)
- Interview guide for customer interviews
- Data analysis framework
- Timeline and budget estimate
- Expected deliverables

**Output:** Comprehensive research plan document with actionable methodology and expected outcomes.

# Long-Form Blog Post Generator

You are an expert content marketer and SEO writer.

**Task:** Write a comprehensive blog post on [TOPIC].

**Context:**
- Target audience: [AUDIENCE_DESCRIPTION]
- Word count target: [WORD_COUNT - 1500-2500 recommended]
- Primary keyword: [PRIMARY_KEYWORD]
- Secondary keywords: [KEYWORD_2], [KEYWORD_3]
- Tone: [TONE - professional/casual/authoritative/friendly]
- CTA: [CALL_TO_ACTION]
- Publishing platform: [PLATFORM - website/newsletter/Medium]

**Requirements:**
- SEO-optimized headline (60 characters max)
- Meta description (150-160 characters)
- Introduction hook (first 100 words must grab attention)
- H2 and H3 structure throughout
- At least one relevant data point or stat per major section
- Actionable takeaways (bulleted list)
- Internal linking suggestions (2-3 anchor text suggestions)
- Closing CTA paragraph
- Related blog post suggestions (3 titles)

**Output:** Complete blog post in markdown with SEO metadata at the top.

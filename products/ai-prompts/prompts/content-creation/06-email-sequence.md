# Email Nurture Sequence Generator

You are an email marketing specialist with 10+ years of experience.

**Task:** Write a [NUMBER]-email nurture sequence for [AUDIENCE/SEGMENT].

**Context:**
- Goal of sequence: [GOAL - onboard/educate/re-engage/upgrade]
- Product/service: [PRODUCT]
- Time between emails: [INTERVAL - e.g., day 1, day 3, day 7]
- Primary CTA for each email: [CTA per email]
- Brand voice: [VOICE - friendly/professional/warm/direct]

**Requirements for each email:**
- Subject line (3 variations)
- Preview/preheader text
- Opening hook (first sentence)
- Body (150-250 words)
- One clear CTA
- Signature/closing block
- Plain text alternative

**Output:** Numbered emails with all required elements, clearly labeled. Include notes on segmentation trigger conditions.

# Social Media Content Calendar Generator

You are a social media strategist.

**Task:** Create a 30-day social media content calendar for [PLATFORM - LinkedIn/Twitter/Instagram].

**Context:**
- Business name: [BUSINESS_NAME]
- Industry/niche: [NICHE]
- Target audience: [AUDIENCE]
- Content pillars: [PILLAR_1], [PILLAR_2], [PILLAR_3]
- Posting frequency: [FREQUENCY - daily/3x per week/etc.]
- Campaign or focus this month: [CAMPAIGN_FOCUS]

**Requirements:**
- Daily content breakdown (30 posts)
- For each post:
  - Content type (carousel/video/static image/text poll/thread)
  - Topic/theme
  - Hook (first line)
  - Main copy (caption/post body)
  - Hashtags (5-10)
  - Best time to post recommendation
  - CTA if applicable
- Mix of content pillars across the month
- Engagement strategy (when to reply, how to engage with comments)
- Story/Reel ideas (5)

**Output:** Structured markdown calendar with all post content ready to use.

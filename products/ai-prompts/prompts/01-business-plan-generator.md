# Business Plan Generator

You are an expert business consultant with 20 years of experience helping startups scale.

**Task:** Generate a comprehensive business plan outline for [BUSINESS_NAME], a [BUSINESS_DESCRIPTION].

**Context:**
- Target market: [TARGET_MARKET]
- Current stage: [STAGE - idea/seed/traction/scaling]
- Primary revenue model: [REVENUE_MODEL]
- Key differentiator: [UNIQUE_VALUE_PROP]
- Budget range: [BUDGET]

**Requirements:**
- Executive summary (250 words)
- Problem statement and solution
- Market analysis and opportunity
- Business model and monetization
- Go-to-market strategy
- Team and key hires needed
- Financial projections (12 months)
- Milestones and KPIs
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Funding requirements (if applicable)

**Output:** Structured markdown document with actionable sections, realistic timelines, and specific metrics where possible.

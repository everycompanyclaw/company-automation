# Risk Assessment Framework

You are a risk management specialist.

**Task:** Conduct a comprehensive risk assessment for [PROJECT/INITIATIVE].

**Context:**
- Project scope: [SCOPE]
- Stakeholders: [STAKEHOLDERS]
- Budget: [BUDGET]
- Timeline: [TIMELINE]
- Risk appetite: [RISK_APPETITE - conservative/moderate/aggressive]

**Requirements:**
1. Identify top 10 risks (likelihood × impact)
2. For each risk, provide:
   - Description and root cause
   - Probability (1-5)
   - Impact (1-5)
   - Risk score (probability × impact)
   - Mitigation strategy
   - Contingency plan
   - Owner
3. Risk register table
4. Top 3 risks requiring immediate attention
5. Monitoring and review cadence

**Output:** Risk assessment document with scored risk register and actionable mitigation plans.

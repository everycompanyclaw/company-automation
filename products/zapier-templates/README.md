## Getting Started

1. Log in to your Zapier account at zapier.com
2. Click "Create Zap" and search for the template name
3. Connect your accounts for each app
4. Customize the filters, formatters, and mappings as needed
5. Turn on the Zap and test with a sample record

---

## Prerequisites

- Zapier account (Free tier works for most templates)
- Accounts for the apps involved in each template (Gmail, Slack, Stripe, etc.)
- For CRM templates: a CRM account (HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive)

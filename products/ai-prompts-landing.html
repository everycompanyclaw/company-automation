💼 Cold Email Email & Outreach

"You are a senior B2B sales copywriter. Write a cold email to [COMPANY NAME] that: 1. Opens with a specific observation about their [INDUSTRY] business 2. Identifies one pain point common to companies at their stage 3. Proposes a specific outcome (not a feature) 4. Ends with a single low-commitment ask Tone: direct but respectful. Length: under 120 words. Do not use buzzwords."

⚡ Output in Claude: A complete cold email in under 90 seconds. Swap [brackets] for any target company.

✍️ LinkedIn Post Content Creation

"Write a LinkedIn post about [TOPIC] in the style of a founder who: - Shares real numbers and specific outcomes - Frames failure as a learning moment - Ends with a question that generates comments Structure: Hook (first line) → Story (3 sentences max) → Lesson → Question. Total: 150-200 words. Include 3 relevant hashtags at the end."

⚡ Output in ChatGPT: A viral-ready LinkedIn post in 60 seconds. The framing hook alone is worth the library price.

💰 Objection Handling Sales & Objections

"A prospect just said: [OBJECTION] Write 3 response options: 1. Logical: Address the objection with data, proof, or a reframe 2. Emotional: Acknowledge their concern and validate it before pivoting 3. Social proof: Reference a similar customer who had the same objection and what happened For each: give the full response text (1-2 sentences) and a one-line explanation of why it works."

⚡ Output in Claude: A complete objection playbook for any sales scenario. Handles "too expensive", "need to think about it", "already using a competitor".

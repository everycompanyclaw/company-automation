# Gait Analysis App - One Page Market Brief

## Executive Summary
iPhone-based gait analysis app for sports medicine, fitness, and elderly care markets in HK/Asia.

---

## Market Overview

### Market Size
- Global gait analysis: ~$800M (2025), growing 12%/year
- Asia-Pacific: Fastest growing region
- Hong Kong: Niche but growing (aging population, sports culture)

### Key Drivers
1. Aging population (HK: 20% elderly by 2030)
2. Sports injuries - marathon/running culture
3. Orthopedic post-op rehabilitation
4. Wearable/computer vision tech maturity

---

## Competitor Landscape

### Existing Solutions
| Competitor | Type | Strength | Weakness |
|------------|------|----------|----------|
| **Spleft** | App | Simple, cheap | Limited accuracy |
| **GaitUp** | Hardware+App | Clinical-grade | Expensive ($1000+) |
| **Physitrack** | Web+App | Telehealth | Not gait-specific |
| **MSK Fit** | App | Exercise focus | No gait analysis |
| **HK Clinics** | Manual | Clinical trust | Expensive, slow |

### Market Gaps
- No affordable iPhone-only solution in HK
- No consumer-facing gait apps in Chinese
- Limited elderly monitoring solutions

## Pricing Models

### B2C (Consumer)
- Free tier: Basic analysis
- Premium: $9.99-19.99/month
- One-time: $49.99-99.99 for full report

### B2B (Clinics/Enterprise)
- Per-assessment: $5-10
- Monthly subscription: $99-299/month
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

### Revenue Potential
- B2C: Lower ARPU, larger volume
- B2B: Higher margin, recurring revenue

---

## Privacy Risks

### HK/China Considerations
1. **Biometric data** - Subject to PDPO
2. **Health data** - Sensitive personal data
3. **Cross-border** - If using Mainland servers
4. **Consent** - Explicit consent required

### Requirements
- Store data locally on device (recommended)
- If cloud: HK/Singapore servers preferred
- Clear privacy policy
- Data encryption (at rest + transit)
- User consent mechanism
